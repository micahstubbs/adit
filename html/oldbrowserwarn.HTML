WebVR is a cutting-edge, experimental technology, and you'll need to install a cutting-edge, experimental web browser if you want to experience Adit in virtual reality. Here's what you'll need to do:
  • Visit https://webvr.info/get-chrome/
  • Click on the "Archive" and then "September, 23 2016" folders to download the correct WebVR version of Chromium (or click here to go directly there)
  • Open Chromium and enable WebVR by enabling the two options below:
  • chrome://flags/#enable-webvr
  • chrome://flags/#enable-gamepad-extensions
